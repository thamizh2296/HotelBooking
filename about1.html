ABOUT

❋

Spice Haven was born in 2010 from a simple belief — that great Indian food should be accessible, authentic, and deeply nourishing. Founded by Chef Arjun Mehta and his family, our kitchen draws on recipes passed down through three generations.

Every dish on our menu tells a story — of regions, rituals, and the remarkable diversity of Indian cuisine. From slow-braised kormas to coastal seafood curries, we bring the full spectrum of flavour to your table.

We are proud to have served thousands of guests, earning recognition as one of the city's favourite Indian restaurants.
